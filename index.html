About Us

Started in 2014, Zugzwang Records was created to create.
We specialize in recording but also offer mixing, mastering, and publishing services.
It's hard to be an artist and maintain the motivation to do what you do.
Our mission is to inspire people to create - and prevent the headaches along the way.

Contact us for more information or to book studio time!
